MEANING

Mosque is a place of worship for Muslims which religion is Islam.It can also refer to as a place set aside for Muslims to worship.The word Mosque is an Arabic word. A larger collective, mosque is refer to as masjid which offers more service to the community.

DESCRIPTION

Mosque can be rectangular or square in shape.The interior and exterior depends on the choice of shape and size. But the simple description of a mosque depends on the following features:
  1. A prayer room with a wall marked with 'Mihrab'which would indicate the direction of Mecca, which Muslims should face when praying.The Prayer Room is usually divided into two segment.The first segment which is the larger segment is for the male and it comes first before the second segment.The other segment is for the female.The praying room consist of mats or rugs

    FRONT VIEW OF A PRAYING MAT

    NOTE:The male segments are usually larger than the female segment because it is compulsory for all males to observe their obligatory solat in the mosque than female
  2. A Dome,called qubba in Arabic.(A symbolic representation).
  3. A place where Muslims purify themselves before observing solat.It consist of taps and sometimes kettle.
  4. A minaret,it a tower which is part of the mosque where a muezzin call Muslims to prayer
  5. some mosque has a compound for parking vehicles
  6. A minibar or pulpit for lecture on Friday.

HISTROY

The first mosque is the one in Mecca,the most holy mosque but the model of early mosque,was the courtyard of Muhammad's house which had a qibla constructed in 622 AD. Mosque grew into becoming more complex and uniform,in their shoes inspired by different persons.

IMPORTANCE

  4. It serve as a meeting place.
